WHO WE ARE

More Than Just a Car Rental

LUXDRIVE was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to provide premium, reliable transportation for everyone. What started as a small fleet of 10 cars has grown into one of Indonesia's most trusted car rental services.

We believe that every journey should be comfortable, safe, and memorable. That's why we invest in maintaining our fleet to the highest standards and training our team to deliver exceptional service.

Our Vision

To become Indonesia's leading premium car rental service, known for exceptional quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where every journey is a premium experience.

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and well-maintained vehicles with transparent pricing and exceptional customer service. We are committed to exceeding expectations at every touchpoint.

Our Journey

2014

Founded

LUXDRIVE was established in Jakarta with an initial fleet of 10 vehicles.

2016

Expansion

Expanded to Surabaya and Bandung. Fleet grew to 50 vehicles including premium SUVs.

2019

Digital Transformation

Launched online booking platform. Introduced monthly corporate packages.

2022

150+ Fleet

Reached 150 vehicles across 15 cities. Served over 5,000 happy customers.

2026

Going Nationwide

Planning expansion to all major cities in Indonesia with an EV-ready fleet.
